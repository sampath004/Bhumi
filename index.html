Rich and vast experience in the field of construction, interior works & real estate.

Bhumi is a construction & real estate services company based in Bangalore with core focus on execution of construction & interior works (residential, commercial, industrial & retail projects) and real estate advisory & transactions.

The company was founded in mid 2012 with an intent and purpose to construct and develop projects which would offer the best in standards in quality, architecture, design and thereby creating and maximizing value for end users and investors.

The partners of Bhumi have rich and vast experience in the field of construction, interior works, real estate transactions. The partners, by leveraging their experience and expertise in the field of construction & real estate are onto creating a unique construction services company.
